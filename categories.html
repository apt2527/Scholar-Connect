Types of scholarships

Understand the different kinds of support available.

Merit-based

Awarded on the basis of marks, ranks or achievements. Ideal for students with strong academic or co-curricular performance.

Need-based

Focused on students from low-income families. Usually requires income proof and basic academic performance.

Government & institutional

Offered by central/state governments, universities and NGOs with specific eligibility and benefits.
